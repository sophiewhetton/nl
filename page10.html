Op Reis Naar Jou

Met dank aan Willy Sommers en voor het vak onderzoek drie zijn wij op reis geweest naar Jou in Spainje.  Het project is nog in opbouw op een vollwaardig documentaire te worden in boek formaat.  Hieronder zie je alvast de fotos met enkele verhalen in het Engels.

At seven am we found ourselves waiting for the bus to La Guingueta in the station of Barcelona Nord. Breakfast was coffee in a polystyrene cup. Nicolas smoked his final cigarette before the five hour drive began. It had been pouring with rain but he managed to maintain his ‘look’ despite a sleepless night in the hostel of Americans turning on lights and tripping over vodka bottles. He hadn’t brought any walking shoes or rain jacket, I cursed over and over that he would suffer on the mountains in his city clothes. I suppose those practicalities had escaped him.
